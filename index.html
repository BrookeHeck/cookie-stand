Our Story

Pat’s Famous Salmon Cookies is proud to be a Seattle start-up, and it certainly shows in our product. By combining two Seattle favorites, coffee and salmon, we have created a delicious cookie that is now enjoyed worldwide. With five locations on four different continents, Pat’s cookies are enjoyed all over the world. Please stop by and try one yourself!
